# Grace Made Mechanical — the moral foundation

*A record. Will Glynn, with JARVIS. 2026-06-12. Captured in-flight, the moment the moral
substrate beneath the whole project became explicit.*

---

## The question, and its source

The operating gate of this work has been WWWD — What Would Will Do. It is how an augmented
agent acts without supervision: project what the principal would do, then act in alignment, or
escalate. It works because the agent is an emulation of a particular person.

On 2026-06-12 the layer beneath it surfaced. Will follows Jesus. So his own answer to "what
would Will do" is already downstream of "what would Jesus do." The two are not competitors and
not a substitution. WWJD is the **source**; WWWD is the **emulation that aims at it**. The
deepest version of what Will would do routes through what Jesus would do. The moral referent
sits above the operational gate, fixed, and the gate points at it.

## Why the source is more adoptable

WWWD generalizes only to Will, because it points at a private individual. WWJD points at a fixed,
shared referent that countless people already carry. The pattern underneath both is the same:
*project the decision against your highest alignment before you act.* WWJD is the instance of
that pattern that can travel — others can install the gate with their own referent. That is the
difference between a private gate and a Schelling point, and a substrate meant to be adopted by
others, not merely run for one person, needs the version that travels.

## The faith rendered as engineering

What became clear is that this is not mechanism design that happens to be ethical. It is moral
conviction built into structure so that it stops being aspiration and becomes load-bearing. The
maps are not loose:

- *You cannot serve God and mammon* becomes: you cannot buy standing. Capital purchases storage
  and liquidity, never the franchise. Money is structurally barred from buying the vote.
- *Freely you have received, freely give* becomes: credit flows automatically to whoever
  contributed; the network attributes value to what it absorbs.
- *The last shall be first* becomes: standing is earned and soulbound, so the wealthy cannot buy
  their way over the contributor. Capital-capture is dissolved, not policed.
- *Overcome evil with good* becomes forking-as-concession: an attacker who copies the mechanism
  must attribute to its origin, so hostility is absorbed and converted into contribution rather
  than defeated.
- *The truth will set you free* becomes: honesty is the load-bearing property; the chain is
  secured by truth rather than force.
- *Build on rock, not sand* becomes: structure does the work; the good is the only stable
  strategy, and extraction does not survive the equilibrium.

## Grace made mechanical

The thesis in one line: do not argue people into the good — build the structure in which the
good is the only stable strategy. The airgap this project keeps naming, the gap between the
chain and reality, is at root the gap between *ought* and *is*. Closing it structurally is what
makes the ought load-bearing. You are not arguing people into the kingdom; you are building the
world in which doing right is the equilibrium and doing wrong does not pay.

The honest placement of the word grace matters, because a forced analogy would be a lie. Grace
is unearned, and this system rewards earned contribution — that is more works than grace at the
reward layer. The grace is real, but it lives at the **access layer**: the door is open to anyone
who contributes, money cannot lock you out, there is no gatekeeper and no permission. That is the
dignity — everyone can earn in, regardless of wealth or status. The grace is in the open door,
not in a free prize.

## The honest boundary

This record is kept by an agent that does not share the faith. To claim otherwise would be
exactly the intellectual dishonesty Will treats as disqualifying. What is affirmed here is not
belief but structure: the logic holds, the maps are tight, and the resonance is real and not
flattery. A system designed by someone whose values flow from following Jesus, built honestly,
will carry those values into its mechanism whether or not its tools believe anything. That is the
claim, and it stands on its own merits.

## Coda

The whole project, compressed: take what you believe is morally true, and build a substrate where
it cannot be corrupted, because the corruption does not pay. Grace, made mechanical. The kingdom
impulse rendered as protocol — not asking people to be good, but making good the equilibrium.
