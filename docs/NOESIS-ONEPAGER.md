# Noesis — one page

**Proof of Mind: a value chain for verified contribution.**

---

**The line.** Bitcoin pays you for burning electricity. Noesis pays you for contributing something useful with your mind — and proves it, so it can't be faked, copied, or bought.

**Why it's new.** Bitcoin built the missing piece that let a market for *trustless ownership* exist. Noesis builds the missing piece that lets a market for *contribution* exist. Same kind of leap, one market over.

**The core move.** The chain becomes its own market. It doesn't ask a committee or a formula what your work is worth — it lets the market reveal it the way every market does: by **what later work actually builds on.** Useful work gets built on and earns; dead ends don't.

**What you earn.** Each contribution is fingerprinted, priced by its real downstream impact, and split into two things:
- a **soulbound standing** (Proof of Mind — reputation + voting weight you earn and can't sell), and
- a **transferable storage credit** you can trade.
> You can buy storage; you cannot buy consensus.

**Why it can't be gamed.** One rule — *pay only for novel work others build on* — defeats every attack: copies earn zero, spam saturates at a ceiling, collusion rings are just value moving in a circle (and the chain sees it), and the learned judge can only lower a score, never invent one.

**Two big claims.**
1. **Cooperation by construction** — cheating isn't punished, it just doesn't pay. The honest move is the highest-paying one, at every layer.
2. **Convergence, not competition** — useful work from any other chain can be imported and re-priced on one shared graph. To every other chain: *not "come compete" — "come join us."*

**Consensus.** Weighted by earned contribution, not money or hardware. Mining/staking only keep the lights on (a liveness floor); they earn no standing.

**Fair launch.** The builder's private head-start is burned to zero at launch, verifiable on-chain. Everyone starts even.

**Honest status.** Built and tested in an open reference node: ownership, pricing, novelty, anti-spam/anti-ring defenses, dispute-and-slash, PoM-weighted finality. The one open problem — judging real-world contribution un-gameably at scale — we name as load-bearing and unsolved, with a measure that adapts faster than it can be gamed rather than a fixed formula that gets gamed on day one.
